# Research expansion checklist

## New market

- Regulator, exchange, disclosure database, investor education and tax authority.
- Trading calendar, timezone, sessions, order/lot/tick rules, limits/halts, settlement and custody.
- Access restrictions, investor classification, ownership limits and currency conversion.
- Corporate actions, accounting standards, filing language and identifier lifecycle.
- Investable benchmark, total-return convention and free-data coverage.

## New asset or product

- Legal claim, cash-flow source, valuation anchor and failure modes.
- Venue, liquidity, fees, taxes, leverage, collateral, custody and counterparty.
- Benchmark, pricing source, roll/decay, tracking error or FX exposure.
- Product documents and conditions that make it unsuitable for beginners.

## New sector

- Value chain, demand units, supply capacity, pricing mechanism and inventory.
- Technology/standards, regulation, geographic concentration and substitution.
- Unit economics, operating leverage, capital intensity and cycle indicators.
- Company mapping by revenue/profit exposure rather than label.

## New free-data provider

- Official or upstream documentation, package/repository version and license.
- Supported markets, instruments, fields, adjustment, timezone and update lag.
- Authentication and rate limits; no shared credentials.
- Fixture tests, schema mapping, provenance metadata, caching, timeout and retries.
- Scheduled live smoke separate from deterministic CI; a fallback provider where lawful.
